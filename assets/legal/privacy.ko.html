1. Controller and Contact Information

Service name: Dear Us — operated by WithStan (individual operator, Republic of Korea).

Email: support@with-stan.com  |  Privacy inquiries: privacy@with-stan.com

Because Dear Us is operated by a single individual, the operator’s identity is not publicly disclosed on this page. If required by applicable law or court order, the operator’s full identity will be disclosed to the relevant authorities.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account data: username (required), profile photo (optional), bio (optional), preferred language.
  • Content data: posts, comments, likes, fan-club messages, media uploads.
  • Communication data: support emails, appeal submissions, user reports.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device & technical data: device OS and version, app version, device token (for push notifications), time zone, locale.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration, interaction timestamps.
  • Crash & diagnostics: anonymized error logs to improve stability.

2.3 Optional Data (Requires Explicit Consent)

  • Precise location: used only when you enable “Nearby Stans”; not stored on servers.
  • Approximate location (region): derived from IP address for content localization; not stored at the country subdivision level.
  • Birthday: optional field used for age-restricted features and personalized greetings.
  • Marketing consent: collected as a separate, optional consent.

We do not collect payment card data. All in-app purchases are processed solely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) under their respective privacy policies.

3. How We Use Your Data

Purpose Legal Basis

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active account: data retained while your account is active.
  • After deletion request: 90-day grace period during which you may restore your account. After 90 days, personal data is permanently deleted from production systems.
  • Backups: deleted from rotating backups within 30 days after production deletion.
  • Legal hold: if a legal obligation or ongoing investigation requires retention, data may be held beyond the standard schedule. You will be notified to the extent permitted by law.
  • Anonymous analytics: aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained indefinitely.
  • Support communications: retained for 3 years from the last interaction for abuse-prevention purposes.

Account deletion is irreversible after 90 days. Exported data packages may be requested before deletion via privacy@with-stan.com.

5. Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements requiring them to protect your data in accordance with applicable law.

6. International Data Transfers

Dear Us is operated from the Republic of Korea. Your data may be processed on servers located in South Korea, the United States, or the European Union, depending on the infrastructure provider.

Transfers to countries outside the EEA are protected by:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable.

Users in South Korea: data is processed under the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). Users in the EEA/UK: data is processed under GDPR/UK GDPR.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (data portability export).
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@with-stan.com. We will respond within 30 calendar days. Identity verification may be required.

South Korean users: additional rights are available under the Personal Information Protection Act. Contact the Personal Information Protection Commission (PIPC) at www.pipc.go.kr if you believe your rights have been violated.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 14 (or the higher age limit required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that personal data of a child under 14 has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child has registered, contact privacy@with-stan.com immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • In-app notification at least 7 days before the change takes effect, or
  • Email notification to registered users, if applicable.

Continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. If you do not agree, you may delete your account.

10. Contact and Data Requests

General support: support@with-stan.com

Privacy requests (access, deletion, portability): privacy@with-stan.com

Response time: 30 calendar days for privacy requests; 7 business days for general support.

Requests must include: your username, registered email, and a description of your request. We may ask for additional verification to protect your account.

2. Datos que Recopilamos

  • Datos de cuenta: nombre de usuario (obligatorio), foto de perfil (opcional), biografía (opcional), idioma preferido.

2.3 Datos opcionales (requieren consentimiento explícito)

  • Ubicación precisa: solo cuando activas “Stans cercanos”; no se almacena en servidores.

No recopilamos datos de tarjetas de pago. Las compras dentro de la app son procesadas exclusivamente por Apple (App Store) o Google (Google Play) bajo sus respectivas políticas de privacidad.

2.2 Dados coletados automaticamente

  • Dados do dispositivo: sistema operacional e versão, versão do app, token do dispositivo (para notificações push), fuso horário, localidade.
  • Dados de uso: páginas visitadas, funcionalidades usadas, duração da sessão, carimbos de data/hora de interação.
  • Diagnósticos de falhas: registros de erros anonimizados para melhorar a estabilidade.

2.3 Dados opcionais (requerem consentimento explícito)

  • Localização precisa: somente quando você ativa “Stans próximos”; não armazenada em servidores.
  • Localização aproximada (região): derivada do IP para localização de conteúdo; não armazenada abaixo do nível nacional.
  • Data de nascimento: campo opcional para funcionalidades com restrição de idade e saudações personalizadas.
  • Consentimento de marketing: coletado como um consentimento separado e opcional.

Não coletamos dados de cartões de pagamento. As compras no app são processadas exclusivamente pela Apple (App Store) ou Google (Google Play) sob suas respectivas políticas de privacidade.

4. Retenção e Exclusão de Dados

  • Conta ativa: dados retidos enquanto sua conta estiver ativa.
  • Após solicitação de exclusão: período de carência de 90 dias durante o qual você pode restaurar sua conta. Após 90 dias, os dados pessoais são excluídos permanentemente dos sistemas de produção.
  • Backups: excluídos dos backups rotativos em até 30 dias após a exclusão da produção.
  • Retenção legal: se uma obrigação legal ou investigação em curso exigir, os dados podem ser retidos além do cronograma padrão.
  • Análise anônima: dados analíticos agregados e não identificáveis podem ser retidos indefinidamente.
  • Comunicações de suporte: retidas por 3 anos a partir da última interação para fins de prevenção de abuso.

A exclusão de conta é irreversível após 90 dias. Você pode solicitar um pacote de exportação de dados antes da exclusão em privacy@with-stan.com.
