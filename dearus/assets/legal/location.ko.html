Article 5. Location-Based Services — Full Korean Law Compliance

5.1 Statutory Compliance Framework

📋 Location Information Act Compliance — Mandatory Disclosures (Art. 16)The Company operates the Safety Check-In feature in full compliance with the Act on the Protection and Use of Location Information of the Republic of Korea (이하 '위치정보법').The following mandatory statutory obligations are affirmed and implemented:• LOCATION INFORMATION MANAGER (위치정보관리책임자): Kim Minjung (dev@with-stan.com) is designated as the Company's Location Information Manager pursuant to Art. 5 of the Location Information Act.• USE/PROVISION LOG RETENTION (이용·제공사실 확인자료): The Company retains a Location Use and Provision Log for a minimum of 6 months pursuant to Art. 16(2) of the Location Information Act. This log contains: service timestamp, anonymized user UID, anonymized recipient UID (partner). Raw GPS coordinates are NOT stored in this log.• CONSENT REQUIREMENT: Location data is collected only upon separate, explicit, affirmative consent obtained prior to first use of the Safety Check-In feature, pursuant to Art. 15 of the Location Information Act.• SEPARATION FROM CORE SERVICE: Refusal of location consent has no effect on the User's ability to access any core Service feature.

5.2 Location Data Processing Details

Data Collected Real-time GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude), movement path, check-in completion time
Purpose Transmitting real-time location and safe arrival notifications to the connected partner only
Recipients The connected partner only — never used for advertising, marketing, analytics, or any other purpose
Legal Basis (Korea) Separate explicit consent under Location Information Act Art. 15
Legal Basis (Brazil) Explicit consent under LGPD Art. 7(I) and Art. 11(I)
Legal Basis (Mexico) Explicit consent under LFPDPPP
Legal Basis (Philippines) Explicit consent under DPA 2012
Withdrawal App Settings → Safety Check-In → Disable. Data collection stops immediately.
GPS Data Retention Auto-deleted within 7 days of check-in completion. Users may delete earlier.
Use/Provision Log 6 months — Location Information Act Art. 16(2). No GPS coordinates in log.

5.3 Limitation of Liability — Location Services

Liability Category Scope
Partner's use of received data The Company has no ability to monitor or control how the receiving partner uses location data after delivery. Not liable in civil law for harm caused by partner's conduct with received data.
Misuse (stalking/coercion) Criminal conduct. Immediate permanent termination + law enforcement referral. Cooperation constitutes the full extent of the Company's legal obligations; it does not constitute admission of civil liability.
GPS inaccuracy or service interruption Not liable for harm from GPS signal instability, network failures, or service interruptions beyond the Company's control.
Design limitations The Company does not warrant that the feature is immune to all forms of social engineering or coercion occurring outside the application's technical controls.

Términos del Servicio Basado en Ubicación

Vigente: 7 de abril de 2026 • Versión 2.0

Artículo 1. Objeto

Estos términos regulan las condiciones de uso del servicio de ubicación ‘Verificación de llegada segura’ de WithStan, de conformidad con la Ley de Información de Ubicación de Corea.

Artículo 2. Contenido del Servicio Basado en Ubicación

La Verificación de llegada segura es una función opcional que transmite su ubicación GPS en tiempo real al único partner de pareja conectado, notificándole su llegada segura.

Artículo 3. Recolección, Finalidad y Retención

Elemento Detalle
Datos recopilados Coordenadas GPS (latitud/longitud), ruta de movimiento, hora de llegada segura
Finalidad Notificación de llegada segura al partner conectado
Retención GPS Auto-eliminado en 7 días
Log de uso/provisión 6 meses (Ley de Información de Ubicación, Art. 16(2))

Artículo 4. Provisión a Terceros

Solo se proporciona al único partner de pareja conectado. No se usa para publicidad, marketing ni otros fines.

Artículo 5. Derechos del Usuario

  • Retiro del consentimiento: Configuración → Verificación de llegada segura → Desactivar
  • Solicitudes de acceso y notificación: privacy@with-stan.com
  • Pausa temporal: desactivar la función dentro de la app

Termos do Serviço Baseado em Localização

Vigência: 7 de abril de 2026 • Versão 2.0

Artigo 1. Objeto

Estes termos regulam as condições de uso do serviço de localização ‘Verificação de chegada segura’ da WithStan, em conformidade com a Lei de Informações de Localização da Coreia.

Artigo 2. Conteúdo do Serviço

A Verificação de chegada segura é uma função opcional que transmite sua localização GPS em tempo real apenas ao único parceiro conectado, notificando-o sobre sua chegada segura.

Artigo 3. Coleta, Finalidade e Retenção

Item Detalhe

Finalidade Notificação de chegada segura ao parceiro

Fornecido apenas ao único parceiro de casal conectado. Não usado para publicidade ou marketing.

Artigo 5. Direitos do Usuário
